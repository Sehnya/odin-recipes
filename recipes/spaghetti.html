Spaghetti Recipe

Yummy!

Ingredients:

  • Ground turkey
  • Spaghetti soodles
  • Ragu sauce
  • 1 whole onion (diced)
  • 1 whole green pepper (diced)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • "Slap Ya Mama" cajun seasoning
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Saute diced onions and green pepper in pan that will be cooking the ground turkey.
  2. Boil Noodles on Med-High heat for 30 mins in separate pot, make sure to add salt and olive oil to boiling water.
  3. Remove noodles from pot and use strainer to separate water from noodles.
  4. Saute Ground Turkey on Med-High heat, iclude all seasonings previously mentioned above.
  5. Once the ground turkey has one unified color, turn down the heat to low, and drain excess oil.
  6. Add Ragu spaghetti sauce of your preference to the cooked ground turkey, onion, and green bell pepper mix.
  7. Mix together until ground turkey is broken up and equally distributed amongst the sauce.
  8. Let simmer for 10-15 minutes to ensure flavors properly blend.
  9. Serve and Enjoy!
